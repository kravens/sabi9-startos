Welcome to Sabi9

Wasabi Wallet on your own node — coins come in, get coinjoined private, and leave without a trace. Here is the whole flow:

1⇲
Receive

Generate an address (with a label of who is paying) and show it as a QR to scan. One address, one use — never reused.

2◆
Coinjoin

Press ▶ on the music box at the bottom of the screen — your coins are mixed with everyone else's until they are private, then it stops by itself.

3⇱
Send

Every send opens a preview with privacy warnings and no-change suggestions before anything is signed.

Creating a wallet gives you 12 recovery words and asks for a password — both together are the only way to restore it. Write them down, in separate places.

Select a wallet in the sidebar to begin — a wallet stays locked until you open it.

Opening wallet…

The daemon is matching block filters and downloading matched blocks over P2P. Balances and transactions appear once it finishes.
